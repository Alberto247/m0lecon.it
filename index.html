Conference

m0leCon 2021 is the third edition of the computer security conference organized by the student team pwnthem0le in collaboration with Politecnico di Torino. The aim is to bring together hackers, security experts, and IT specialists from different backgrounds and skill levels. The conference will feature a variety of talks and presentations, mostly focused on various aspects of offensive security; it will be completely free of charge and entirely held in English.

This year's edition will take place on December 4 from 10:00AM at Politecnico di Torino, Aula Magna "Giovanni Agnelli". The entry for the attendees is Corso Duca degli Abruzzi 24, Torino.

Call For Presentations

Call for Presentations is open! m0leCon is a computer security conference, so any work related to computer security will be considered by the program committee. We encourage CTF-related or in general offensive security works with live demonstrations to the audience. Submission does not have to be completely original, but works with higher grade of novelty will be preferred. The committee will evaluate the submissions in terms of:

  • relevance
  • novelty
  • completeness
  • technical content

Every presentation will be 30 minutes long, plus an additional 5-10 minutes slot for questions. The presentation must be in english. Travel and accommodation costs will be covered by the organizers, plus a speaker pack with gadgets and gifts from the conference. Call for Presentations will be open from 20th of July 2021 to 17th of October. Notifications for the accepted presentations is due to October 31st. Early submissions may receive earlier notifications. In order to apply, you should send an email to info@m0lecon.it with subject [CfP m0leCon 2021] <your title here>, including

  • Your name
  • Your contancts (including twitter/personal website)
  • Where you plan to travel from (if accepted)
  • A short biography (max 1500 chars)
  • The abstract (max 4000 chars)
  • An outline of the talk (max 8000 chars)
  • Any additional material (slides, papers, demos)
  • If the work is novel (otherwise, where and when it has been presented)

m0lecon CTF

The main CTF is aimed to experienced CTF teams and will be played on-site. This CTF will be limited to 12 teams, including the 5 winning teams of our Teaser CTF:

  • C4T BuT S4D
  • c0r3dump
  • Bushwhackers
  • WreckTheLine
  • AlPray

The other spots will be reserved for teams of conference attendees. More info on how to register coming soon.

m0lecon beginner CTF

The beginner CTF is aimed to students and newcomers, with very easy to mid-level challenges. It will be fully online on Friday December 3rd, and open to an unlimited number of teams of at most 4 people. Top student teams from Politecnico di Torino will be awarded during the conference on the next day. Registrations will open a few weeks before the CTF day.
